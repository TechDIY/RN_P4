    slender adjective
    uk ​ / ˈslen.də r / us ​ / ˈslen.dɚ/

    C2 thin and delicate, often in a way that is attractive

    苗條的，纖細的
    He put his hands around her slender waist. 他雙手摟住了她纖細的腰。
    The plant's leaves are long and slender. 這株植物葉子細長。

    › small in amount or degree

    少量的
    a man of slender means (= without much money) 窮人
    The chances of settling this dispute through talks seem increasingly slender. 透過對談解決這場爭端的機會似乎越來越渺茫。
